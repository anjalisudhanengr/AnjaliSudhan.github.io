Developed a machine learning model to predict the 2022 FIFA World Cup winner, involving data preprocessing, model selection, training, evaluation, and deployment, leveraging FIFA rankings and historical match data. Predicted the winning team of FIFA World Cup 2022 successfully via data analysis, pre-processing, model selection, evaluation and deployment.
